Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 30, 2026

1. Overview

Welcome to Neron's Hub. I value your privacy. This page explains what data is collected and how it is used. Short version: I don't track you for creepy reasons.

2. Analytics (GoatCounter)

I use GoatCounter to see how many people visit my site. This is a privacy-first tool. It does not use cookies, it does not collect personal information, and it does not track you across different websites.

3. Advertising (EthicalAds)

This site intends to show advertisements through EthicalAds. Unlike most ad networks, EthicalAds does not track you or use "behavioral targeting." They show ads based on the content of the page (like coding or gaming) rather than your personal search history.

4. Third-Party Links

My site contains links to other websites (like Buy Me a Coffee). Once you leave my site, their privacy policies apply.

5. Contact

If you have questions, feel free to reach out via the contact form on the main page.
